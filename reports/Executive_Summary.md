## Business Overview

Nassau Candy Distributor generated **$141,784 in total revenue** and **$93,443 in gross profit** in FY 2025, achieving an exceptional overall gross margin of **66.5%**. The company operates 15 products across 3 divisions (Chocolate, Sugar, Other), serving customers in 4 US regions.

| # | Finding | Impact |

| 1 | **Chocolate division posts 67.5% average gross margin** — among the highest achievable in candy distribution | $88,825 gross profit (95% of total company profit) |

## 3 Things Requiring Immediate Action

| # | Problem | Risk | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|---|

## Key Recommendation for Leadership

> **Nassau Candy Distributor has a highly profitable core business in its Chocolate division. The strategic priority is twofold: (1) protect that core from concentration risk, and (2) eliminate margin-destroying products like Kazookles that dilute overall portfolio quality.**

Removing Kazookles and actively scaling the top three Sugar products (Everlasting Gobstopper, Hair Toffee, Fizzy Lifting Drinks) would improve average portfolio margin by an estimated 1–2 percentage points and reduce SKU-level operational complexity.
